### 1. Total Sales by Product & Segment
A stacked bar chart compares sales across multiple products (Paseo, VTT, Amarilla, Carretera, Velo) and customer segments (Channel Partners, Enterprise, Government, Midmarket, Small Business). The visual is interactive and provides detailed sales and profit insights for each product.  
**Insight:** Paseo generates the highest overall sales.

### 2. Sales & Profit Over Time
A dual-axis line chart displays Sales and Profit trends for 2014. The visual is fully interactive and can be filtered by product and segment.  
**Insight:** Trends vary depending on the selected product and customer segment.

### 3. Profit by Country
An interactive map visual highlights profitability across global regions, helping identify top-performing countries. The visual allows users to filter and explore product-level sales and profit by country.
